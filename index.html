BACKGROUND

Hailing from a small farm town called Orting, I come from a family of six, the third of four children. My hobbies include drawing, exercising, reading, and

I came into the VCD program in 2012 with a background in Studio Art. Graphic design was not at the forefront of my mind as far as future plans went. As a former Developmetal Psychology major, there was a deep-seated desire for creative freedom led me to the Visual Communication Design program.

I am currently completing my bachelor's in VCD, wrapping up my education at Eastern Washington University in Winter 2015.

I'm a former illustrator and graphic artist who worked for the college paper, The Easterner. The areas that I tend to do well in include information design and logo design/branding. The areas I'm really trying to focus on is understanding and practicing responsive web design, and overcoming shyness when presenting my work.

OBJECTIVE
